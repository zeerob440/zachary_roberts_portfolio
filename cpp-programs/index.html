Early Stages of Learning C++

At this point in my engineering career, I need to learn a compiled language. That compiled language is C++. I strongly feel passion drives innovation. Since C++ is widely used in entertainment, I believe it will continue to outpace other compiled languages into the intermediate future in both entertainment and enterprise. I am also intrigued with it because it excels at both procedural and object-oriented programming. I understand learning it will be a challenge, but nothing worth doing is easy, and I will have a greater range as an engineer for it with C++.
