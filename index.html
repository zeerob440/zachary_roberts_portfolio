Zachary Roberts, CHEP

I am a curious systems-level thinker transitioning from Healthcare Emergency Management to Software Engineering. I discovered a passion for software engineering when I was assigned to automate a rules-based emergency mass notification system at a regional healthcare system. I designed and implemented critical notification templates which supported hospital incident response. I enjoyed watching this system work under adverse emergency conditions; it created a bridge of logic over a sea of chaos to improve the lives and realities of others. I design software with the guiding principles of reliability, resiliency, and user experience.
